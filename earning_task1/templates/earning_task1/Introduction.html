In Part 2 of the experiment, you will be asked to make a series of decisions in three periods.

At the start of each period, you will be asked to perform a task to determine your earnings for the rest of that period.

You will then be randomly matched with three other participants in each round. Together you will form a decision group. You and the other three participants will be asked to choose a tax and transfer scheme for your decision group. From these choices, the computer will randomly select two and apply it to all participants in the decision group.

The next section goes through these procedures in more detail.
