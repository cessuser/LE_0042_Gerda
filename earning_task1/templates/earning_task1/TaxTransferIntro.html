After the Earnings Task, you will be randomly matched with three other participants in each round to form a decision group. Everyone, including you, will see the group membership and the earnings of each participant in the decision group.

The decision group consists of participants with 800, 600, 400, and 200 tokens.

All participants start the round with their earnings, except for the participant with 400 tokens. The earnings of this participant also depend on a competition between them and the participant with 200 tokens. More specifically, this competition entails a comparison of the raw points that each of them collected in the Earnings Task.

If the difference between the number of raw points earned by the participant with 400 tokens and those earned by the participant with 200 tokens is large, the participant with 400 tokens wins the competition. This means that they will keep their earnings.

However, if the difference is small, the participant with 400 tokens loses the competition. This means that this participant will lose 40 tokens of their earnings.
